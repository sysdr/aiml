Key Checks

Statistical Expectations

  • Bernoulli: proportion ≈ p
  • Binomial: mean ≈ n·p, std ≈ √(n·p·(1−p))
  • Normal: mean ≈ μ, std ≈ σ
  • CLT: means of uniforms → Normal
  • Calibration: overconfident vs well-calibrated distributions
