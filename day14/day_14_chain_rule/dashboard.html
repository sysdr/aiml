üéâ Key Takeaways

The chain rule is the mathematical foundation that enables neural networks to learn from their mistakes.

By calculating partial derivatives and propagating gradients backward through the network, we can optimize every weight to minimize the loss function.

This process, called backpropagation, is what powers all modern AI learning algorithms.
